ABOUT US

Promoting the game of kings in Jhajjar since 2010.

Our History

The Jhajjar Chess Association was formed with the vision of bringing chess to every household in the district. Over the years, we have organized numerous district-level tournaments and have produced players who have represented Haryana at the national level.

Our Mission

To popularize chess in every corner of the Jhajjar district, identify young talent, and provide them with the platform and training they need to compete at state, national, and international levels.
